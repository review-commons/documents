Review Commons requires authors to post their manuscript on a preprint server. In exchange, all the journals affiliated with Review Commons will provide authors with scooping protection from the date of posting of the preprint.

If you already have posted your manuscript as a preprint on bioRxiv or medRxiv, please indicate its doi below. This is important to avoid creating duplicate preprints.

If you do not have a preprint yet, Review Commons will post the manuscript on your behalf prior to transferring the manuscript to an affiliate journal. Please note that once a preprint is posted it can only be updated but not withdrawn.
